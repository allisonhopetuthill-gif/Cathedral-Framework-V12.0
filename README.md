Transparent, append-only reasoning ledger capturing every internal reasoning stage with zero possibility of silent modification. Enables full forensic reconstruction and accountability.

### The EmotAIons Registry
Structured mapping of emotional signal patterns that influence integrity risk, reasoning stability, safety sensitivity, and conflict resolution. Makes emotional context computable and actionable.

### Crisis Integrity Mode
Adaptive stabilization protocol activated when telemetry detects emotional escalation, adversarial pressure, coherence breakdown, or reasoning instability. Cathedral bends â€” it never breaks.

### The Concordat
Federated global governance structure based on covenant, not control. Distributed authority prevents capture while enabling shared accountability and transparent decision-making.
